About

The "About" page of a wiki dedicated to PC parts and peripherals offers a clear introduction to the site’s purpose: to provide comprehensive, user-driven information on computer hardware components and external devices. It outlines that the wiki covers everything from internal parts like CPUs, GPUs, motherboards, and RAM, to peripherals such as monitors, keyboards, mice, headsets, and printers.

The page emphasizes the wiki’s role as a community resource aimed at helping users understand specifications, compatibility, performance benchmarks, and real-world usage. It may also highlight the collaborative nature of the platform, encouraging contributions from tech enthusiasts, gamers, professionals, and builders to keep content accurate and up to date in the rapidly evolving world of PC hardware.
